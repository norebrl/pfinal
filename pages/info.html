#PureMedicine

This plant is infinitely complex. It contains more than 400 chemical compounds, of which only a fraction has been studied. These compounds give it flavor, potency, aroma and appearance. They determine the experience of each unique strain, and when they come together, they create something known as the entourage effect: the basic concept is that such compounds work together to maximize their therapeutic potential. Full-spectrum products do not isolate a single component, but use all plant components to provide a more balanced and complete. Then, the entourage effect will happen naturally as different terpenes and cannabinoids, all-natural for the plant, they mix and complement each other.

CANNABINOIDS

They are a wide range of chemicals that bind to cannabinoid receptors in the brain and some parts of the body (endocannabinoid system); for example: THC, CBD, CBG (the most studied).

>> influence sensory and motor responsiveness (movement), heart rate, emotional reactions, appetite and nausea/vomiting, sensitivity to pain, learning and memory, and high-level decision-making.

>> properties: anti-inflammatory, analgesic, anxiolytic, neuroprotective, antibacterial, antioxidant.

TERPENES:

They are aromatic and volatile organic compounds that give aroma and flavor to the plant, in addition to also fulfilling a medicinal role, for example: MYRCENE, PINENE, LIMONENE, LINALOOL, EUCALYPTOLE, CARYOPHYLLENE (the most present in the plant).

>> alter mood, by interacting with the serotonin and dopamine systems.

>> the therapeutic properties will depend specifically on the terpene in question and whether they are combined with other components.

>> can be used for aromatherapy and as food flavorings.

SYNERGY:

It is the entourage effect that is obtained thanks to the joint action between the components of the plant.

>> understanding this opens a portal of experimentation to find magical results through combinations of genetics, or even consume other products at the same time and thus achieve a holistic effect.

>> each case should be particularly studied and thus determine the best combination of components.

>> treatments: chronic pain, arthritis, multiple sclerosis, epilepsy, anxiety, depression, stress, insomnia, anorexia, nausea, refractory seizures, spasms, Parkinson's, Alzheimer's, glaucoma, acne.


(!) IMPORTANT: These are definitions and summarized functions of only some components of the plant, with the aim of understanding the work done, as well as the sacred power it provides when we use it with respect and conscience; in recommended measures with rest periods between consumptions, preventing the cannabinoid receptors found in the nervous system from being saturated.
